Example Facility Safety Pulse (mock data)

This page shows a mock “Safety Pulse” view for a fictional facility. It is here to illustrate how different pieces of public information could be combined into a single, readable snapshot — not to represent any real-world location.

In a live system, these views would be built from actual state and CMS data, refreshed regularly, and clearly annotated with data sources and update dates.

Safety Pulse overview

Staffing trend: Stable RN coverage on weekdays, slightly lower on weekends, no prolonged gaps in the past 12 months.

Inspection history: Recent inspection with a small number of non-harm-level deficiencies, all noted as corrected.

Incident signals: Occasional state-reportable events, but no recent pattern of repeated harm-level incidents.

Regulatory actions: No current payment denials or active enforcement actions in the last reporting period.

How a Safety Pulse view could help you

A view like this does not replace a visit, conversations with staff, or advice from professionals. Instead, it might help you:

  • Decide which facilities to visit first.
  • Prepare more focused questions about staffing, incidents, or changes in ownership.
  • Monitor whether patterns are improving, staying stable, or becoming more concerning over time.

In the future, Maisage could allow you to track facilities over time, create shortlists, and receive simple alerts when public information changes in meaningful ways.
