VA benefits and support for veterans

Veterans and their families may have access to programs that can help with health care, long-term care, and other supports. Understanding what exists is the first step; figuring out which programs apply to your situation often requires direct contact with VA or accredited advisors.

This page offers a high-level orientation, not a complete list of programs or eligibility rules.

Types of VA-related support

Depending on service history and other factors, veterans may have access to:

  • VA health care services.
  • Long-term care options through VA or contracted facilities.
  • Programs that may help with in-home care or caregiver support.
  • Disability compensation and pension programs, in eligible cases.

Each program has its own criteria and application process. Official VA resources and accredited representatives are key for up-to-date details.
