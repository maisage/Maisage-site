Neutral care-navigation project (early stage)

Care decisions are hard. The information around them shouldn’t be.

Maisage helps families sort out the basics: what’s really happening, what choices are on the table, and what questions to bring to doctors, facilities, and advisors — without trying to sell you a policy or a placement.

Understand the basics

You can use everything on this site on your own, or add optional one-time sessions with Mai Lindsey if you want structured help.

What you can use Maisage for today

This early version focuses on a few high-impact areas families struggle with the most:

  • Getting a clear snapshot of what’s going on at home.
  • Comparing “stay home vs. move” scenarios.
  • Looking past marketing when researching facilities.
  • Understanding which medications drive cost and complexity.

The goal is not to tell you what to do — but to help you see your options clearly and walk into conversations prepared.
