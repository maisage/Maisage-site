Transparency • Independence • Dignity

Guidance you can trust — for the years ahead, not just the next crisis.

Maisage helps you cut through confusing information about Medicare, Medicaid, housing, long-term care, and support services. The goal is simple: give you clear, unbiased information so you can make your own plan, stay independent longer, and bring your family into the conversation before decisions fall into crisis mode.

What is Maisage?

Maisage is not a placement service and not a referral site. It doesn’t earn money from facilities, pharmacies, or insurance plans. Instead, it is built to act as a neutral guide — helping you understand how the systems around aging actually work, where costs really sit, and what tradeoffs come with different choices.

You can use Maisage to learn about Medicare and Medicaid, see how medication pricing really works, compare types of care (from in-home help to nursing facilities), and map out realistic paths for your own situation. Your family can follow along, but the focus starts with you: your values, your preferences, and the way you want your later years to look and feel.
