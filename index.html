Care decisions are hard. The information around them shouldn’t be.

Maisage helps families sort out the basics: what’s really happening, what choices are on the table, and what questions to bring to doctors, facilities, and advisors — without trying to sell you a policy or a placement.

Understand the basics

You can use everything on this site on your own, or add optional one-time sessions with Mai Lindsey if you want structured help.

What you can use Maisage for today

This early version focuses on a few high-impact areas families struggle with the most:

  • Getting a clear snapshot of what’s going on at home right now.
  • Comparing “stay home vs. move” and mixed care scenarios.
  • Looking past glossy marketing when researching facilities.
  • Seeing which medications may be driving cost and complexity.

The goal is not to tell you what to do, but to make it easier to see your options and walk into real conversations prepared.

What makes Maisage different

This project exists because many caregivers are tired of hidden incentives, rushed decisions, and information that feels more like marketing than truth.

No commissions, no referral fees

Maisage is not paid by facilities, insurers, pharmacies, or brokers. If money ever comes from partnerships in the future, it will be disclosed clearly and prominently.

Plain language, not jargon

Tools, summaries, and checklists are written so a tired family member can understand them at 11 p.m. after a long day — not just professionals.

Designed to work with your care team

Everything here is meant to make conversations with doctors, pharmacists, attorneys, and financial planners sharper — not to replace them.
