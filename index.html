What Maisage can help you with

Use Maisage as a planning companion when you’re trying to answer questions like:

  • “Can we realistically keep Mom at home for 2–5 years?”
  • “What are we not seeing in this facility’s glossy brochure?”
  • “Are there lower-cost options for these medications?”
  • “How will this choice affect our finances over time?”

Our tools surface patterns, trade-offs, and questions to ask — you stay in control of the decisions.

How Maisage fits into your decisions

Think of Maisage as a quiet, neutral partner that helps you see the whole board before you commit.

1

Get oriented

Use Start Here to summarize your situation: who needs care, current diagnoses, living setup, time pressure, and financial boundaries. This becomes your “one-page snapshot.”

2

Look at facilities & options

Use Facility Insights to see inspection patterns, staffing and ownership trends, and complaint history so you know where to dig and what questions to ask on tours.

3

Map out costs and medications

Use the medication transparency and planning tools to understand which meds drive cost, and how different care paths (home, assisted living, nursing facility) might play out financially over time.

4

Decide with your own team

Bring what you learn to your doctors, pharmacists, care managers, and legal/financial advisors. Maisage gives you better questions and clearer trade-offs — not a sales pitch.
