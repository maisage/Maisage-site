Community & Volunteers

Building a community of clarity, care, and support

Maisage is not just a website — it's a community of people who want aging to be clearer, calmer, and more human. Whether you're someone planning for your own future, helping a loved one, or simply interested in improving how families navigate aging, there’s a place for you here.

Become a Maisage Navigator

Navigators are volunteers who help gather information, share experiences, or help identify real gaps in the system. You don’t need credentials or certifications — only honesty, commitment, and a willingness to help others find clarity.

  • Help review local resources and agencies in your area.
  • Contribute insight about housing, home care, or facility quality.
  • Share experiences that may help others avoid confusion or crisis.
  • Assist in gathering publicly available data for the Living Map.
