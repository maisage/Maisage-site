Learn about Medicare, Medicaid, care options, and real-world costs

This section is about clarity. Each topic below focuses on explaining how a part of the system works — Medicare, Medicaid, medications, hospice, housing, and more — in plain language, using publicly available information.

Nothing here is personalized advice or a recommendation. The goal is to help you understand the structure of your options so you can ask better questions, plan ahead, and decide what fits your life and your values.
