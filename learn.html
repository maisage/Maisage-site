Learn how Medicare, Medicaid, care settings, and costs really work

This section is about understanding the systems around aging — not selling you a plan or steering you toward any provider. Everything here is based on publicly available information and plain-language explanations. The goal is to help you see how things fit together so you can make clearer decisions.

These pages do not replace legal, financial, or medical advice. They are meant to give you a starting point for questions and conversations with your family and your professional advisors.
