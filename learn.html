Medicare – where to look and what to watch

Medicare covers hospital and medical care for people 65+ and some younger adults with disabilities. It is essential, but it has limits — especially for long-term care.

Official sources:

  • Medicare.gov – Main site
  • What Medicare Covers
  • Premiums and Costs

Key things to pay attention to:

  • Medicare does not cover long-term custodial care.
  • Advantage plans may limit networks and require approvals.
  • Out-of-pocket costs vary widely by plan.

Medicaid – eligibility, spend-down, estate recovery

Medicaid can help pay for long-term care once income and assets meet eligibility levels. Rules vary widely by state and change over time.

Official sources:

  • Medicaid.gov – Federal overview
  • Your state Medicaid site (“[State] Medicaid long-term care eligibility”).

What to pay attention to:

  • Income and asset rules.
  • Differences between home care and facility coverage.
  • Estate recovery requirements after death.

Maisage does not interpret state law — we highlight what families should review carefully.

Hospice – what most families don’t know

Did you know?

Hospice care does not cover room and board. It pays for comfort-focused care, medications, equipment, and nursing visits — but not the place where someone lives.

When hospice is provided in assisted living, memory care, or a nursing facility, the facility’s monthly charges still apply separately.

This is one of the most common — and costly — misunderstandings families encounter at the end of life.

Official source: Medicare.gov – Hospice Coverage

Medicines – comparing prices and avoiding surprises

The same medication can cost very different amounts depending on pharmacy, payment method, and insurance rules. Maisage does not sell or promote medications — we show you how to compare safely.

Examples of places to compare prices:

  • Major pharmacy price-check tools
  • Discount programs listing cash prices
  • Manufacturer assistance program sites

Questions to ask:

  • Brand vs generic?
  • Cash price vs insurance price?
  • Is another pharmacy offering a lower price for the same quantity?

Maisage does not endorse specific pharmacies — we help you compare in a transparent way.
