Learn how aging, care, and cost systems really work

This section is for education only. Each topic explains, in plain language, how a part of the system works — medication pricing, Medicare, Medicaid, care settings, and more. The goal is to give you a clearer map, not to tell you what to choose.

Information is based on publicly available sources like Medicare.gov, state agencies, VA resources, and consumer materials. Programs and rules change over time — always verify details through official sources before making decisions.
