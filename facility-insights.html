Safety Pulse ‚Äì how the color indicators work

Safety Pulse is a simple traffic-light summary of public data patterns. It is not a score, grade, or recommendation. It is meant to guide where you look closer and what questions you ask.

üü© Green ‚Äì Generally stable

Public indicators show no unusual patterns. You should still visit, ask questions, and trust your own impressions.

üü® Yellow ‚Äì Mixed signals

Some patterns in inspections, staffing, complaints, or enforcement suggest a closer look.

üü• Red ‚Äì Elevated concern

Repeated or recent issues in public data. This does not mean ‚Äúavoid,‚Äù but it does mean careful, specific questions and follow-up.

In a live dashboard, these indicators would be recalculated when new inspections, staffing reports, or enforcement actions are published.

How to use this state view

This state-level mock is meant to show how Maisage could help families understand patterns before they get lost in details. It is not labeling any facility or region as good or bad.

  • Use it to decide where you may want to spend more time reviewing facilities.
  • Combine these patterns with your own visits, conversations, and instincts.
  • Remember that every facility is unique, even within the same county or city.
