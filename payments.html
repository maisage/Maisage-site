What happens after you pay

  1. You’ll receive a payment confirmation email from the payment provider (for example, Square).
  2. Maisage will receive notice of your payment and the service you selected.
  3. You’ll receive a follow-up email from Maisage within one business day to schedule your session or confirm details.

For questions about services before paying, please visit Services or reach out via the Contact page.
