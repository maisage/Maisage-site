Payments & Donations

Maisage is designed to be simple and transparent. You are the only paying party: there are no commissions, referral fees, or product markups. Payments are for time spent researching, organizing, and explaining publicly available information so you and your family can make clearer decisions.

All examples and structures below are for information only until Square payment links are activated.

Your Care Path Session

A structured 60–90 minute working session to look at your current situation, worries, and goals, and to outline realistic paths for the years ahead.

  • Clarify your priorities (independence, money, location, family roles).
  • Review key systems: Medicare, Medicaid, facilities, home support, benefits.
  • Compare possible care paths in plain language.
  • Outline simple next steps you can share with family.

When Square is enabled, this will be a flat-fee payment (for example: Your Care Path Session – $TBD). No extra charges without prior agreement.

Additional research & project-style work

Some situations need deeper research: multiple facilities, complex benefit combinations, or ongoing support over several weeks. In those cases, Maisage can extend beyond the initial session on a clear, time-and-materials basis.

  • Facility comparison with inspection patterns and ownership history (public data).
  • Benefits mapping across Medicare, Medicaid concepts, VA programs, and housing resources.
  • Structured follow-up calls to refine your plan with new information.
  • Help organizing documents, checklists, and “family explainer” summaries.

Before any extended work starts, you’ll agree on the scope, estimated time, and payment structure. No automatic renewals, no hidden charges.

Voluntary donations (optional)

Some people may wish to support the ongoing development of Maisage’s free guides, tools, and facility transparency work. Small donations – even as little as $1 – help cover the time spent turning public data into plain-language resources.

Donations are always optional, never expected, and completely separate from any paid sessions or project work.

What happens after you pay

Once payment links are enabled, the general flow will look like this:

  1. You complete payment securely through Square on a hosted checkout page.
  2. You receive a confirmation email/receipt directly from Square.
  3. You are prompted to:
    • fill out the Start Here form, or
    • schedule a call time (details will be confirmed by email).
  4. You and Maisage confirm the scope and next steps in writing before deeper work begins.

If anything ever feels unclear, you can pause and ask questions before moving forward.
