Services

Practical help for navigating aging, care, Medicare, and planning

Maisage helps you understand your options and turn them into a clear plan. You stay in control — Maisage provides structure, research, plain-language explanations, and support to help you make decisions that fit your goals.

All services use publicly available information. No legal, financial, medical, or plan-selection advice is provided.

How pricing works

A simple structure: clear fixed prices for core work, plus optional time-and-materials (T&M) if you need deeper research.

  • Core Navigation Session — $150: A structured working session + written summary + one follow-up clarification.
  • Family Aging Plan / Written Plan — $75: A shareable PDF summarizing your situation, goals, and options.
  • Additional Research (T&M) — $75/hr: Only billed if you ask for extra work. Always pre-agreed with a clear scope and a cap. No surprise hours — ever.

These numbers are examples and can be adjusted later if your services evolve.

Navigation & Research Services

Core navigation session – clarify your situation & map options

A structured session to get your situation out of your head and into a clear, usable outline.

What’s included ($150):

  • Pre-session intake: A short optional questionnaire so we start with the basics already captured.
  • One focused session (~60 minutes): Clarify goals, identify 2–3 realistic care paths, and talk through trade-offs.
  • Plain-language written summary: A recap of your situation, scenarios, and questions to ask next.
  • One follow-up clarification: A short email reply if something in the summary needs clarification.

Uses only public information. No plan recommendations or legal/financial advice.

Medicare Options & Plan Map

A one-on-one session to help you understand Medicare’s structure and how each path fits your doctors, medications, travel, and financial picture.

  • Compare Original Medicare + Medigap + Part D with Medicare Advantage in clear terms.
  • Review how retiree coverage, VA benefits, or Medicaid change your choices.
  • Receive a plain-language map you can take to SHIP, HR/benefits, or plan reps.

Usually fits within the $150 core session; deeper comparisons may use T&M with a pre-agreed cap.

Medication & cost research

Help understanding why medication costs vary so widely and where the biggest price differences come from.

  • Compare insurance, cash, discount, and mail-order pricing.
  • Explain markup layers in plain language.
  • Create a simple medication cost comparison summary.

Often fits in one session; deeper research billed T&M if you request it.

Facility deep-dive & shortlist review

A closer look at facilities you’re considering using public inspection, staffing, complaint, and ownership data.

  • Create a shortlist of facilities by ZIP/city.
  • Summarize inspection patterns and red flags.
  • Provide tailored questions to ask on tours or calls.

Mix of core session + optional T&M depending on how many facilities you want reviewed.

Care path & budget planning

Plain-language scenarios showing how different care paths might play out over the next several years.

  • Outline 2–3 paths (home-first, hybrid, facility-first, Medicaid path, etc.).
  • Estimate costs and timing at a high level.
  • Highlight when savings may run out and what that means.

Often needs a core session + a bit of T&M modeling (pre-agreed with a cap).

Family Aging Plan (shareable summary)

A written PDF summarizing what matters most to you, the care paths you're considering, and key questions to revisit as circumstances change.

  • Goals and non-negotiables.
  • Summary of your care paths.
  • Checklist of questions to revisit each year.

Fixed fee: $75 per plan PDF.

Focused topic research

When you need deeper digging on a specific question using public sources.

  • Clarify the question you’re trying to answer.
  • Summarize public sources in plain language.
  • Provide links to original documentation.

T&M billed at $75/hr, with scope and maximum hours agreed in advance.

Ongoing navigation check-ins

Periodic touchpoints to adjust plans as health, finances, or family support changes.

  • Quarterly or semiannual check-ins.
  • Review changes and update scenarios.
  • Maintain alignment with your goals.

Can be a small recurring fee or occasional T&M.
