Services rooted in clarity, care, and realism

Your Care Path Session – understanding your options with clarity and care

A warm, structured conversation to help you see where you are, what paths are realistic, and what comes next — without crisis pressure.

What’s typically included for $150:

  • Pre-session intake: A short optional questionnaire so basic details are captured before we talk.
  • One Care Path Session (~60 minutes): Clarify your goals and non-negotiables, identify 2–3 realistic care paths (home-first, hybrid, facility-first, etc.), and talk through trade-offs in plain language.
  • Plain-language written summary: A recap of your situation, the paths we discussed, and key questions to ask next (facilities, agencies, doctors, SHIP, or advisors).
  • One follow-up clarification by email: A brief written clarification if something in the summary isn’t clear.

Uses only public information and your own details. No legal, tax, financial, or medical advice, and no recommendations for specific products, plans, or facilities.

Medicare Options & Plan Map

A dedicated Care Path Session focused on Medicare — helping you understand how the main paths (Original + Medigap + Part D vs. Medicare Advantage) intersect with your doctors, travel, medications, and income.

  • Walk through the Medicare Options Map from the Learn page in the context of your life.
  • Consider how retiree coverage, VA benefits, or Medicaid may affect your choices.
  • Receive a short written map you can take to SHIP, HR/benefits, or plan representatives.

Often fits within Your Care Path Session pricing; deeper comparison work can use T&M if you request more detailed research.

Medication & cost research

Help understanding how medication pricing works in practice and where the biggest cost differences may be for your current prescriptions.

  • Compare insurance, cash, discount program, and mail-order options using public information.
  • Explain the main “layers” that affect what you pay in plain language.
  • Provide a simple cost comparison summary you can use with your prescriber or family.

Often starts as a Care Path Session with follow-up written work; deeper research can be T&M if you choose.

Facility deep-dive & shortlist review

A closer look at facilities on your short list, using public inspection, staffing, complaint, and ownership data where available.

  • Create or refine a shortlist of facilities based on location and basic criteria.
  • Summarize inspection and complaint patterns to highlight where to look closer.
  • Provide a tailored list of questions to bring to tours, calls, or meetings.

Often a mix of Your Care Path Session plus agreed T&M research depending on how many facilities you want reviewed.

Care path & budget planning

Scenario-style planning to see how different care paths might affect your finances and options over the next several years.

  • Outline 2–3 realistic care paths (home-first, hybrid, facility-first, Medicaid path, etc.).
  • Estimate high-level cost and timing impacts for each scenario.
  • Highlight when savings may be strained and what that might trigger.

Usually combines a Care Path Session with some T&M modeling, always with a pre-agreed cap.

Family Aging Plan (shareable summary)

A written PDF you can print or email to family, capturing what matters most to you and the paths you are considering — to reduce confusion later.

  • Your goals, values, and non-negotiables.
  • A summary of your care paths and key assumptions.
  • A short checklist of questions to revisit each year.

Fixed fee: $75 per plan PDF. Often prepared after Your Care Path Session once directions are clearer.

Focused topic research

When you need deeper digging on a specific question that can be answered with public information and clear explanation.

  • Clarify the question and how you will use the answer.
  • Collect and summarize relevant public sources in plain language.
  • Provide links back to original materials so you can verify and share.

T&M billed at $75/hr, with scope and a maximum hours cap agreed in advance.

Ongoing check-ins & support

Light, periodic touchpoints to adjust your care path as health, finances, or family support change.

  • Quarterly or semiannual check-ins.
  • Review what has changed and whether your plan still fits.
  • Keep everyone aligned without waiting for a crisis.

Can be structured as a small recurring fee or occasional T&M, based on your preference.
