Services

Services designed to help you make informed, confident decisions

Maisage offers fee-only guidance. No commissions. No sales. No incentives to steer your choices. You pay only for the time, research, and support you request.

Paid Navigation Sessions

The primary service Maisage offers is one-on-one navigation support for families who want help understanding options, comparing costs, and planning next steps. These sessions are structured as professional services — not sales conversations.

What’s included

  • Medication cost research and comparison using publicly available data
  • Provider search (care facilities, home care, hospice, etc.) with no referrals or incentives
  • Care planning — mapping realistic paths and timelines
  • Understanding Medicare, Medicaid, hospice, home health, private duty, and care levels
  • System navigation — knowing who to call and what to ask
Learn more about Navigation Sessions

Optional services (available by request)

These services may be offered on an as-needed basis. All work is based on publicly available information and neutral analysis.

Customized Planning Reports

A written summary of long-term care pathways, cost considerations, and next steps based on your goals. Fee based on time required.

Medication Cost Comparison Summary

A structured comparison using publicly available pricing tools to help families identify more affordable medication options.

Care Options Exploration Session

A session dedicated to exploring care levels (home support, assisted living, memory care, skilled nursing), timelines, tradeoffs, and cost planning.

No medical records needed

Meaningful guidance can be provided without sharing personal health records. You can keep things general: age, living situation, goals, and concerns.

Any personal details shared are voluntary and should be limited to what you are comfortable with. Maisage does not request patient portal access or store medical information.
