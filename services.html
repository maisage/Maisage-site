Is this a fit for your situation?

These sessions may be helpful if:

  • You’re juggling input from multiple family members and professionals and need a clearer mental map.
  • You want someone neutral to help you think through trade-offs without steering you toward a specific product or facility.
  • You’re tired of feeling rushed into decisions without enough time to understand the consequences.

They’re probably not a good fit if you’re looking for:

  • Urgent crisis response (for example, a medical emergency right now).
  • Specific legal, tax, financial, or medical advice.
  • Someone to act as a broker, agent, or placement service.
IMPORTANT
Maisage sessions are for orientation and planning support only. They do not provide medical care, diagnosis, treatment, legal advice, financial planning, tax guidance, or insurance brokerage. All decisions should be reviewed with licensed professionals who understand your specific situation and local rules.
