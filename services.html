Your Care Path Session

A structured 60–90 minute working session where we review your situation, concerns, and goals. Together, we outline realistic pathways for the years ahead.

  • Clarify your priorities (independence, finances, family roles, health).
  • Review Medicare, Medicaid basics, facilities, home care, benefits, and safety.
  • Map realistic options based on your preferences.
  • Outline clear next steps you can share with family.

Facility Support

  • Researching nursing homes, assisted living, memory care, and senior apartments.
  • Calling facilities to check Medicaid bed availability and current admission status.
  • Reviewing inspection history (public information).
  • Checking ownership changes and complaint patterns (when available publicly).
  • Plain-language summaries of risks, red flags, and questions to ask.

Benefits & Applications

  • Medicare basics, enrollment windows, and plan differences.
  • Medigap vs. Medicare Advantage explanations.
  • Understanding Medicaid rules (eligibility, spend-down, estate recovery).
  • VA Aid & Attendance (publicly available rules only).
  • Social Security retirement, SSDI, and SSI guidance.
  • Low-income senior housing directories.
  • Utility assistance programs.
  • Property tax reduction / freeze programs for older adults (state-dependent).

Medication & Medical Cost Support

  • Medication cost research using public discount programs and price benchmarks.
  • Explaining the pharmacy supply chain in simple terms.
  • Researching medical equipment options and public programs that help pay for them.

Daily Living Support

  • Home caregivers (non-medical) and companion care options.
  • Meals, grocery delivery, housekeeping, laundry, and other home-support services.
  • Lawn care, minor home repairs, and safety improvements (public programs included).
  • Transportation options for older adults.

Technology, Safety & Connection

  • Help with phone/tablet setup for reminders and communication.
  • Simple safety technology (cameras, sensors, alerts) — explanation only.
  • Setting up shared calendars or reminders with family.
  • Safe social media use without confusion or scam risks.
  • Strategies for staying connected without feeling overwhelmed.

Safe & Enjoyable Travel

  • Accessible travel options that match your mobility, stamina, and comfort needs.
  • Helping identify questions to ask before booking trips or cruises.
  • Planning realistic itineraries with rest days and backup options.

Life Administration & Organization

  • Helping organize bills, accounts, and regular payments (non-financial control).
  • Budgeting basics using publicly available tools.
  • Document checklists for safe recordkeeping.
