Services & Payments

Services to help you make sense of your options

Maisage is built around clear information and calm planning. When you’re ready for more personal help, these services are meant to give you focused support — without selling products, taking commissions, or steering you toward any particular provider.

All sessions and work are based on publicly available information and your goals. Nothing here is legal, financial, or medical advice — it’s guidance to help you see your options more clearly so you can make informed decisions.

Your Care Path Session

A one-on-one conversation focused on your situation — where you are now, what you’re worried about, and what you want your next years to look like.

Together, we walk through:

  • Your current situation and what you’re trying to solve.
  • Major care and support options that realistically fit your context.
  • How Medicare, Medicaid, private pay, and family support can interact.
  • Ways to stay independent longer with practical supports.
  • How to bring family into the conversation without waiting for a crisis.

After payment, you’ll receive an email within 24 hours to schedule your session.

Family Aging Plan (PDF)

A concise written summary of your goals, care paths, cost expectations, and next steps — something you can review, share with family, and update.

A Family Aging Plan can include:

  • Your goals and preferences for independence, care, and living setting.
  • Coverage notes (Medicare, Medicaid, private pay, VA where applicable).
  • Possible care paths with rough cost and support implications.
  • Questions to ask providers or facilities.
  • Suggested topics for early family conversations.

After payment, you’ll receive an email within 24 hours to begin the plan.
