Tools

Tools to plan, compare, and prepare

These tools don’t decide for you. They help you see the trade-offs in front of you — costs, workload, risks, and how well each option fits what matters most to the person at the center of decisions.

Fill out Start Here first Browse all tools

If everything feels urgent, Start Here is usually the best first step. If you’re already focused on a decision, you can jump straight into a specific tool.

How these tools are meant to help

They’re designed for families to use on their own — in plain language, printable, and neutral.

  • Compare stay vs. move and short vs. long-term paths.
  • Map out family workload and strain.
  • See where medication lists and costs are getting out of control.
  • Bring better questions to facilities and medical appointments.

You can use one tool at a time or a few together as you clarify next decisions.
