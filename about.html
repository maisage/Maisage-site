Why Maisage exists — and who it’s for

Maisage was created to give families clear, grounded guidance for aging, care, and end-of-life decisions — without sales pressure, hidden incentives, or confusion.

The Founding Story

Maisage began from a deeply personal place — born out of love, exhaustion, and the realization that the aging support system often leaves families to fend for themselves.

I watched my mother, my mother-in-law, and my husband’s brother face the end of life while navigating paperwork, inflated costs, and incomplete information. Families weren’t failing their loved ones — the system was failing the families.

“When the money runs out, Medicaid will take over.”

This belief has become the unspoken plan for aging in America — but Medicaid steps in only after independence is already gone. Decisions then fall to people who may barely know you.

Maisage exists to change that pattern — helping you stay in control longer and design an ending that reflects your values.
