Common questions

Do I have to book a paid session to contact you?

No. You’re welcome to email or call with a brief overview of your situation first. From there, we can decide together whether a paid navigation session would actually be helpful.

What kind of information can you help with?

I focus on understanding options and costs: programs like Medicare and Medicaid, levels of care, what hospice or home health typically cover, and how different choices affect independence and spending. Everything is based on public information and plain-language explanation.

Will you tell me which doctor, facility, or pharmacy to use?

No. I don’t make specific provider recommendations or receive any referral fees. Instead, I help you understand how to compare options, what questions to ask, and how to spot cost or coverage issues.

Do I need to share medical records or detailed health information?

No. Useful guidance can be provided using general information about age, living situation, and goals. You should only share what you’re comfortable with, and Maisage does not request access to medical portals or records.
